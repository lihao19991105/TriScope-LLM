# dualscope-first-slice-result-package

## Purpose / Big Picture

Package the first-slice rerun results into a clear, auditable summary that distinguishes real artifacts, validated capability probes, placeholders, and limitations.

## Scope

### In Scope

- Summarize final setting, capability mode, Stage 1/2/3 outputs, evaluation, costs, and limitations.
- State what can and cannot be reported.
- Produce one verdict and recommendation.

### Out of Scope

- No new experiment execution.
- No fabricated performance metrics.
- No full matrix.
